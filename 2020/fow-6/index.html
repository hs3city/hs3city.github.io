Future of Work

Future of Work #6

Hackerspace Trójmiasto and Toptal invite you to join the Future of Work online event - a conversation about changs in the work environment, professional development, and best practices in the workplace.

We are a community of brilliant professionals: developers, designers, remote workers, freelancers, coaches, founders, entrepreneurs, and everyone in between. If you are curious about work transformation and professional growth, these talks are for you!

For the 6th edition of Future of Work, we have asked the speakers to share their thoughts on making the best of and adapting to the current employment conditions. While they might not be the most favorable ones, it doesn’t mean that you can’t thrive in your job. Join us on July 15th, 2020 to learn about it firsthand.

This is an open conversation, so there will also be an opportunity to ask questions and talk to speakers and the Hackerspace Trojmiasto community.

Speakers

Kevon Cheung

Co-Founder and CEO of Toasty

Kevon is the co-founder and CEO of Toasty and he is passionate about enhancing virtual interactions between people. Everyone believes that physical meetings and workshops are always better than virtual ones, but Kevon thinks it could be the opposite, which is how Toasty was started.

Twitter Kevon Cheung Linkedin Kevon Cheung

Frédérique Mittelstaedt

Software engineer and entrepreneur

Frédérique is a software engineer and entrepreneur with an MSc in theoretical physics from Imperial College London. He excels at building full-stack systems with the web, desktop and mobile apps, microservices, and external integrations. Frédérique co-founded an international marketing agency and three start-ups in developer tools, cybersecurity, and AI. He regularly releases TypeScript packages and contributes to other open-source projects.

Twitter Frédérique Mittelstaedt Linkedin Frédérique Mittelstaedt

Bartosz Król

Senior Software Engineer at X-Team

Currently contracting for Riot Games, creating systems for millions of players. Fascinated with Serverless Architecture, Functional Programming enthusiast and a devoted Tabletop RPG Player. Oh and has a podcast too.

Twitter Bartosz Król
